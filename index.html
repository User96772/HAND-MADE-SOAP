About Neem Soap

Our handmade neem soap is crafted with care using natural ingredients. Neem is known for its skin-friendly properties and has been used for centuries for its numerous benefits.

Ingredients

  • Organic neem oil
  • Coconut oil
  • Shea butter
  • Essential oils for fragrance
  • Distilled water
  • Sodium hydroxide (lye) for saponification

Benefits

Neem soap is known for its antibacterial and moisturizing properties. It helps in maintaining clear and healthy skin while providing a refreshing bathing experience.

How to Use

Wet the soap and lather on your body. Rinse thoroughly with water. For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes.
